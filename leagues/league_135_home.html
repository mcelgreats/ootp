Philadelphia Wins Top Spot in National League Eastern Division
Saturday, October 1st , 2016
The Philadelphia Phillies have won the National League Eastern Division.

"We made it. Everything we talked about doing in spring training has come true so far," said Rob Noce. "We're not done though, there's still a larger goal in mind."

Philadelphia has a record of 87-73 record and will by vying for their 1st League Series. It was the club's 3rd National League Eastern Division title.

"You never get sick of celebrating something like this," said starting pitcher Pete Alexander.
No Late-Season Magic Coming: New York Out of Playoffs
Saturday, October 1st , 2016
The hopes of lifting a championship trophy in New York this year have now vanished. The New York Mets have been eliminated from postseason contention.

"I'm not happy but I'm trying to come to terms with it," starting pitcher Dwight Gooden said.

New York hasn't yet made it to the postseason. They haven't made the playoffs in all 3 years of their history.
5-Hit Game for Fernandez
Saturday, October 1st , 2016
Tony Fernandez really sizzled at the plate in his team's 12-2 win over the Boston Red Sox. The Toronto shortstop blistered Boston for 5 hits in 5 at-bats.

"Fernandez was too tough to get out today," the Red Sox skipper said in the postgame interview. "No matter what we threw him, he hit it. And he hit some good pitches, too."

Tony Fernandez singled in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 2nd, walked in the 4th, doubled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

This year, Fernandez is hitting .282, produced 62 RBIs and 11 home runs, scored 77 times and gotten on base at a .334 rate.
