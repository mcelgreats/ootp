First Baseman Nabs NL Rookie Award
Saturday, October 1st , 2016
The National League Rookie of the Month Award for September goes to Raúl Rodríguez, the first baseman for Milwaukee. He took the league by storm, hitting .291 and looks like he might just be a legitimate contender for the Rookie of the Year Award.

Rodríguez tagged 30 hits in 103 at-bats, including 6 home runs, produced 17 RBIs and scored 15 times.

This season, Rodríguez is hitting at a .240 pace with 31 home runs, 81 RBIs and has scored 76 times.
