Maloney Garners NL #1 Pitcher Award
Saturday, October 1st , 2016
A standout performance in September earned Jim Maloney the Pitcher of the Month Award in the National League. When his name was penciled in to start by the Cincinnati skipper, it meant trouble for opposing teams. For the month the Reds star racked up 4 wins and 1 loss.

In 38.1 innings of work, Maloney limited opposing batters to 22 base hits and a .168 batting average. He notched 44 strikeouts, issued 18 walks and had a 1.88 ERA.

This season, Maloney has 10 wins and 11 losses and a 3.80 ERA.
