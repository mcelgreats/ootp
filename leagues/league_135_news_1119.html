NL Player of the Week Named
Monday, September 26th , 2016
The baseball must have looked as big as a balloon to Matt Kemp of Los Angeles last week. The center fielder had quite a 6-game stretch and today he was named the National League Player of the Week.

Kemp delivered 10 hits in 20 at-bats to hit .500 with 3 home runs and 11 RBIs.

For the season, Kemp has produced 160 hits in 563 at-bats for a .284 average with 28 home runs and gotten on base at a .338 clip.
